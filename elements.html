Client Checklists & Protocols

Immediate Response Steps

  • Isolate affected network segment.
  • Contact CySafe Incident Hotline.
  • Do NOT power down affected systems.

CySafe Charter Principles

  • Zero Tolerance for Data Resale.
  • Absolute Client Confidentiality.
  • Full Legal Defense of Privilege.

Onboarding Checklist (Yearly)

  1. Review current threat landscape.
  2. Verify Secure Vault credentials.
  3. Audit access logs (last 90 days).
  4. Update disaster recovery plan.
  5. Run stress test on network firewall.
  6. Verify all endpoints are patched.

Key Solution Definitions

Sentinel AI

Our proprietary real-time threat analysis engine that learns from global intrusion attempts and adapts your network defense automatically.

Privacy Protocol

A comprehensive architecture focusing on Data Minimization, Zero-Trust Segmentation, and mandatory encryption for all customer data.

Secure Vaults

Contextual-access encryption containers used primarily by our Legal sector clients to protect attorney-client privilege documents.
